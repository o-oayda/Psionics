# Mind Blank, Psionic
*6th-level Voice*\
**Manifesting Time:** 1 action\
**Range:** Touch\
**Cost:** MB 6, PD 11\
**Duration:** 24 hours\
**Requirements:** None

As the \spell{mind blank} spell, except as
noted here. You conceal your mind---or the mind of one creature
you touch---from outside influence, rendering it effectively
invisible.
