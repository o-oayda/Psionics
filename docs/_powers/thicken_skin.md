# Thicken Skin
*1st-level Prana Bindu*\
**Manifesting Time:** 1 action\
**Range:** Self\
**Cost:** MB 1, PD 1\
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes\
**Requirements:** None

Your skin or natural armour thickens and spreads
across your body, providing a +2 bonus to your AC.
