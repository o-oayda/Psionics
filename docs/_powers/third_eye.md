# Third Eye
*5th-level Prescience*\
**Manifesting Time:** 1 action\
**Range:** Self\
**Cost:** MB 5, PD 9\
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute\
**Requirements:** None

Your vision awakens to those that were hitherto
concealed from you. You gain \emph{truesight} out to 120 feet.
